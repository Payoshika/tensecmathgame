10 Seconds Math Game

Game Rule

1. you have 10 seconds to answer. questions

2. when you got the right answer, 1 second will be added to your clock.

3. There are 4 types of calculations. In the default Setting, only "add" is selected. you can add as you like

4. You can choose max number up to 50. Default setting is 10.

5. Point algorithm.
when your answer is right, you get 10 points. if you chose the more difficult setting, you get more point per answer.
factor1: max number. if you chose 20 as max number, you get 20 points per answer. if you choose 50, you get 50!(however, "add" is always 10 points per answer) factor2: calculation types.you get the below weighting factor based on the calculation type of each question.
add:100% (default)
subtract:120%
multiply:140%
divide:140%
